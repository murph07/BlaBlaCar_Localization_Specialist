Carpool, bus, and 2025 train booking now sit side by side in one app. Every new mode and market is another set of strings that has to read native.

The role asks someone to keep the run reliable while the AI and strategy work moves in parallel. That space between fast and reliable is the whole job.

What I'd want to learn first.

Three things I'd map before I touch the live loop.

Week 1

How the AI-to-linguist handoff actually flows: where strings get staged, who reviews them, and where they tend to stall.

Week 2

Which validation flags fire most in Phrase, from placeholders to plurals to do-not-translate terms, and the usual culprits by locale.

Week 3

The monthly product content review: who the per-locale reviewers are, and how their changes get collected and applied back.
